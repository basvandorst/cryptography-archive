Thank you for your interest in Security Dynamics’ ACE/Agent for Netscape Servers v1.1. Please read all the documentation associated with this product before you enable any of its features.

How to use the ACE/Agent Administration Interface

  • To take full advantage of the features of this interface, make sure you have JavaScript enabled in your browser's preferences.
        
  • Click the Administer ACE/Agent button to open the ACE/Agent Administration applet.
        
  • Click the Index button to open an alphabetical list of related topics.
     Note: The hyperlinks in the Index window display their targets in the right-hand frame.
       
  • Click the Glossary button to open an alphabetical list of key terms associated with ACE/Agent for Netscape Servers v1.1.
    Note: Sometimes the Glossary window will "hide" behind the main browser window. If you click the Glossary button and nothing seems to happen, use your browser's Window menu to make sure the secondary window is not open already.
       
  • Your browser’s Back and Forward buttons display pages in the right-hand frame’s history list. (A history list references a hierarchy of pages you've already viewed.)
       
  • There are also hyperlinks within each topic page that jump to related topics. These hyperlinks are marked with a .
        
  • If you would like to print a topic, click anywhere in the right frame, then select Print Frame from your browser’s File menu.
