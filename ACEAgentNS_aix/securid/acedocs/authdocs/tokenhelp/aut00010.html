Obtaining PIN Information from Your Administrator

Before you attempt to log in for the first time, copy the text below and paste it into an email message to your administrator. Ask the administrator to fill in the relevant PIN information and then email it back to you.

If you have any questions contact your security administrator, __________________________, at extension ______________.

__ The system will assign a PIN to you; you cannot create your own.

__ You will be allowed to use a PIN that you make up yourself.

__ Your PIN may contain letters as well as digits.

__ All PINs on the system must be the same length: ____ characters.

__ Your PIN may contain from ____ through ____ characters.

__ You will have a duress PIN.

__ You are required to access protected URLs through https URLs.
