GPhone v1.0.1

Date: 25-5-1996

GPhone is a simple security tool that enables private person to person
conversations over Intranets and the Internet using IDEA cryptography
and GSM voice encoding technology.

A new feature allows the caller to dial a regular phonenumber within
the server area to complete a private person to person call.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at marcel@comsat.iaehv.nl

NOTE: This software is ment as a toy and should therefore not be used
      for professionall purposes.

Available for FreeBSD, Linux and HP-UX. (HP-UX not well tested)
