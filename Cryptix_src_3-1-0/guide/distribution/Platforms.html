Supported Platforms

§ Cryptix was developed and tested most heavily using the Sun JDK on the platforms marked with a § (FreeBSD, Solaris, W95/NT). The distribution of V3 has been produced on FreeBSD, Solaris and Linux, whatever was to hand at the time (and fastest).

Where Sun JDK 1.1 is indicated, all variants are expected to work well (although not every variant was necessarily tried).
