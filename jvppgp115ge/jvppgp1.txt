PGP-Frontend and Pegasus Mail Extension for Win95/NT 4.0 using a special pgp (2.6.3i)-dll

jvppgp is a frontend for pgp, including:
- complete key management
- encryption
- decryption
- configuration management

jvppgp can handle files or clipboard. It supports drag and drop.
You can work with several keyrings.

jvppgp is freeware!
